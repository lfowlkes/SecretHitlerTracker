Game Log:

This is where you can keep track of what has happened already!

Normal State:
Use the arrows to select which three cards were drawn by the president.
Click on the card that was played in this round.
When ready, press confirm to enter your selections.

Conflict State:
On the left side of the screen, use the arrows to select which three cards the president drew.
Click on the card that the president claims to have discarded.
On the right side of the screen, use the arrows to select which two cards the chancellor claims to have received.
Click on the card that the chancellor played.

Random State:
Similar to the normal state, use the arrows to select which card was obtained.
This state is mainly useful for the enactment of a policy through the election tracker.
